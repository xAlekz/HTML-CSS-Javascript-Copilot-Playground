Queso Helado: A Creamy Peruvian Delight

Experience the creamy, sweet flavors of Queso Helado, a beloved Peruvian dessert. This simple recipe will bring the flavors of Peru straight to your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: Your favorite fruits, nuts, or chocolate chips for topping

Instructions

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.
  2. Chill: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to chill completely.
  3. Freeze: Pour the chilled mixture into a freezer-safe container and freeze for 4-6 hours, or until firm.
  4. Serve: Scoop the frozen mixture into bowls and top with your desired toppings. Serve immediately.

Tips

  • For a smoother texture: If you have an ice cream maker, you can churn the mixture for a creamier texture.
  • Customizations: Add your favorite flavors to the mixture, such as cocoa powder for a chocolate flavor or fruit purees for a fruity twist.
  • Storage: Store any leftover Queso Helado in an airtight container in the freezer.
